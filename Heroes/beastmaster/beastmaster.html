STRENGTH

BEASTMASTER

SUMMONS BEASTS TO AID HIS HUNT

Summoning a poison-spitting boar and a scouting hawk to aid his hunt, Beastmaster never stalks the battlefield unprepared. Chopping through the forest with his axes, his stunning roar opens the way for his allies to devastate enemies.

ATTACK TYPE

MELEE

COMPLEXITY

ABILITIES

WILD AXES

Beastmaster sends his axes flying and calls them home again, slicing through enemy units and trees along their path. Each axe can hit an enemy once, and amplifies subsequent damage from Beastmaster and his units.

CALL OF THE WILD BOAR

Beastmaster calls forth a Boar to aid in the battlefield.

CALL OF THE WILD HAWK

Beastmaster sends an invisible Hawk to scout an area.

INNER BEAST

Untaps the inner fury of nearby allies, passively increasing their attack speed.

PRIMAL ROAR

Beastmaster lets loose a deafening roar that stuns, and shoves open a path to its target. All units in the path of the roar are damaged, while units shoved aside by the roar have their movement and attack speed slowed. Additionally, Beastmaster and his units gain 40.0% movement speed for 3 seconds.

STRENGTH

BRISTLEBACK

TAKES REDUCED DAMAGE WHEN FACING AWAY FROM ENEMIES

When it comes to a fight, Bristleback really puts his back into it. Reducing all damage arriving from the rear or from the sides, he wades into battle with barely a care, spraying enemies with damaging quills and slowing snot.

ATTACK TYPE

MELEE

COMPLEXITY

ABILITIES

VISCOUS NASAL GOO

Covers a target in snot, causing it to have reduced armor and movement speed. Multiple casts stack and refresh the duration.

QUILL SPRAY

Sprays enemy units with quills dealing damage in an area of effect around Bristleback. Deals bonus damage for every time a unit was hit by Quill Spray in the last 14.0 seconds.

BRISTLEBACK

Bristleback takes less damage if hit on the sides or rear. If Bristleback takes 210.0 damage from the rear, he releases a Quill Spray of the current level.

HAIRBALL

Coughs a quill-packed hairball towards the target location. Erupts at the location, hitting enemies in a 700 radius with Goo and 2 Quills.
